Esther Fallick

Esther Fallick (She/her) is a comedy icon, cabaret icon, and transfemme icon based in Brooklyn, NY.

Her three main passions are performing, fostering community, and receiving compliments. She combines these passions at Heart O' The City, her monthly variety show and clothing swap where she spotlights Brooklyn's best trans/gender non-conforming talent.

Under her deadname, Broadway World called her “deeply hilarious”. Post-transition, Broadway World called her “intriguing”! Also recently, Vulture called her “Funniest Internet Videos of October”.

She's a musical comedian but get this: she actually has a really great musical theater BFA (Go Blue!). Her solo shows have played UCB & 54 Below.

Resume and writing samples available upon request.
